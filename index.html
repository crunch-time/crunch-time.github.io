For students

Students use CrunchTime to browse their school's courses, generate conflict-free schedules, and save their favorites. Custom events can be added to accommodate for extracurriculars or a part-time job.

For universities

CrunchTime is an open source, whitelabel application free for universities. See the deployment guide for hosting instructions. Have questions? Join our public Slack group or file an issue.
